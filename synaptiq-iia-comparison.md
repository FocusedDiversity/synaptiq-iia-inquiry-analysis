## Partnership Opportunity Analysis

### Potential Synergies

| Scenario | IIA Contribution | Synaptiq Contribution |
|----------|------------------|----------------------|
| Client needs full journey | Baseline Assessment → Strategy | Implementation → Deployment |
| Client has strategy, needs build | Warm handoff | Technical delivery |

### Referral Triggers

**IIA → Synaptiq:**
- "Client completed assessment, ready to build"
- "Client needs computer vision / ML implementation"
- "Client in healthcare/legal/construction needs technical partner"
- "Client wants to build AI into their product"

**Synaptiq → IIA:**
- "Client needs large-scale organizational assessment"
- "Client struggling with analytics adoption/culture"
- "Client needs leadership training/coaching"
- "Fortune 500 client needs enterprise-wide maturity evaluation"

## Competitive Positioning Notes

### When Synaptiq Competes (vs other implementers)
- Emphasize: Custom solutions, healthcare depth, computer vision expertise, ethical AI
- De-emphasize: Enterprise-wide organizational transformation

### When IIA Competes (vs other advisors)
- Emphasize: Independence, research depth, expert network, Davenport credibility
- De-emphasize: Technical implementation capabilities

### When Both Could Serve
- **AI/Data Strategy engagements** - IIA for organizational focus, Synaptiq for technical focus
- **Readiness assessments** - IIA for cultural/adoption lens, Synaptiq for technical/capability lens
- Position as complementary based on client's primary pain point
