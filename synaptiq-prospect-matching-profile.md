## Qualification Questions for Discovery Calls

1. **Industry:** What industry are you in?
   - *Best fit: Healthcare, Legal, Construction, Government, Financial Services*

2. **Data Type:** Do you have unstructured data (documents, images, video) that needs processing?
   - *If yes: Machine Vision, OCR, LLM opportunities*

3. **Maturity:** Are you looking for AI strategy guidance or ready-to-build solutions?
   - *Strategy: AIQ Assessment, AI & Data Strategy*
   - *Build: ML Development, AI Agents, Machine Vision*

4. **Systems:** Do you have legacy systems that need modernization?
   - *If yes: System modernization projects*

5. **Visual Data:** Is computer vision (quality control, detection, recognition) relevant?
   - *If yes: Strong fit - 9 computer vision projects*

6. **Data Silos:** Do you need to unify data across multiple sources?
   - *If yes: DataLake solution*

7. **Automation:** What manual processes are costing you the most time?
   - *Identifies AI Agents, RPA, automation opportunities*

8. **Product:** Are you building AI into a product vs. internal operations?
   - *Product: MVP development, ML model development*
   - *Operations: Process automation, analytics*

## Red Flags / Poor Fit Indicators

Proceed with caution if prospect:

- Needs only off-the-shelf SaaS products (not custom development)
- Has no data or very limited data assets
- Expects fully autonomous AI with no human oversight
- Industry has no alignment with case study portfolio
- Budget expectations misaligned with custom consulting
- Looking for staff augmentation only (not project-based)
- No executive sponsorship for AI initiatives
