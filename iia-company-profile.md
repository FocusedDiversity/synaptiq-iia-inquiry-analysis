# International Institute for Analytics (IIA) - Company Profile

> **Purpose:** Reference profile for understanding IIA's services, expertise, and market positioning as an analytics advisory firm.

---

## Company Overview

**International Institute for Analytics (IIA)** is an independent research and advisory firm founded in 2010 by Jack Phillips and Thomas H. Davenport. Based in Portland, OR, IIA specializes in helping enterprises transition to data-driven decision-making and advanced analytics.

**Mission:** Help organizations build strong analytics programs and make the transition to advanced analytics and AI.

**Track Record:**
- 200+ organizations served globally since 2010
- 30,000+ maturity surveys conducted
- 300+ annual expert 1:1 sessions
- Network of 150+ analytics experts

**Headquarters:** 851 SW Sixth Avenue, Suite 1025, Portland, OR 97204

| Service | Description | Engagement Type |

## Ideal Client Profile

**Best fit organizations:**
- Large enterprises (Fortune 500 / enterprise scale)
- Organizations struggling with analytics adoption/maturity
- Companies needing objective assessment of AI/analytics readiness
- Leadership teams seeking strategic roadmap before implementation
- Enterprises with analytics programs that aren't delivering expected ROI

**Typical engagement triggers:**
- "We've invested in analytics but aren't seeing results"
- "We need to assess our AI readiness"
- "Our analytics team isn't aligned with business priorities"
- "We need a roadmap for becoming data-driven"
- "We want an independent audit of our analytics program"
